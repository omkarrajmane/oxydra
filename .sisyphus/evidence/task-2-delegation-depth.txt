KEY FINDINGS
================================================================================

Session ID Chain Format:
- Parent: "parent_session"
- Child: "subagent:parent_session:<uuid>"
- Grandchild: "subagent:subagent:parent_session:<uuid>:<uuid>"

Depth Calculation:
- Depth = count of "subagent:" prefixes in session_id
- Level 1 (child): 1 prefix
- Level 2 (grandchild): 2 prefixes
- Level N: N prefixes

Context Propagation:
- parent_session_id: chains correctly through levels
- parent_user_id: preserved through delegation
- agent_name: resolved from agent definitions
- goal: passed through delegation request

Cancellation Propagation:
- CancellationToken passed to delegate() call
- RuntimeDelegationExecutor uses parent_cancellation in run_session_for_session_with_tool_context()
- Cascade cancellation through the entire chain

Max Delegation Depth:
- Tested depths: 3, 5, 6, 10
